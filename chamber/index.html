About the Chamber

The Mubende Chamber of Commerce is the voice of business in Mubende Municipality. We bring together local enterprises (from hardware suppliers and medical clinics to hotels and tech startups) to foster economic development, advocate for pro-business policies, and create networking opportunities.

Our members enjoy exclusive benefits, including business promotion, access to training, and a strong community of like-minded entrepreneurs.
